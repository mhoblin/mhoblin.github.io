Most of my work is fine art and expressive design, but lately I have been dabbling in more serious, professional styles and I am really enjoying the outcomes. My style tile represents this interest since I am using very neutral colors and clear, legible and readable fonts. I want to be perceived as a professional, so I designed my logo to portray just that. It is clean, reproducable, and has a unique icon - a hedgehog. The hedgehog makes my logo a little more personable since it gives the logo a more friendly (cute animal) appearance. Because even the hedgehog is designed thoughtfully, the animal doesn't ruin the professional look.
